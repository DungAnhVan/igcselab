## 4. Diagnostic Landing Page Copy

H1: IGCSE Diagnostic Test for Math & Science Students

Subtitle: Find out exactly where your child is struggling before starting another tutoring class.

Core copy:
- Before more lessons, understand the pattern behind the mistakes.
- The diagnostic session checks how the student thinks through questions, uses formulas, reads graphs, handles units, and responds to command words.
- Parents receive a weak point summary, subject gap map, recommended study roadmap, and suggested learning priority.

CTA: Book a Diagnostic Session

Microcopy: We will contact you to arrange a suitable diagnostic time. No long-term course commitment is required.

Disclaimer: IGCSE Lab is an independent tutoring and diagnostic learning service. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Cambridge Assessment International Education or any examination board. IGCSE is used only to describe the curriculum and exam preparation support offered.

## 8. Suggested UI Design System

- Colors: navy `#0B1F3A`, blue `#2563EB`, light blue `#EAF2FF`, soft grey `#F5F7FA`, dark text `#111827`, accent green `#10B981`, plus amber and rose accents for subject variation.
- Type: system sans-serif stack for fast loading and clean academic readability.
- Components: sticky header, subject cards, diagnostic map, process cards, FAQ details, lead form, CTA band.
- Layout: mobile-first responsive grids, 8px radius cards/buttons, light academic background grid, calm spacing.
- Visual asset: generated diagnostic workspace image saved at `assets/img/igcse-diagnostic-workspace.png`, with a compressed WebP version used on the homepage.

## 10. Technical SEO Checklist

- Unique SEO title and meta description per core page.
- One H1 per page.
- Canonical URL per page.
- Schema added: EducationalOrganization, Service, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage, Blog, ItemList.
- `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` included.
- Three real article pages included in `sitemap.xml`.
- Internal links between service pages and the diagnostic landing page.
- Image alt text included.
- Mobile-first responsive CSS.
- Lightweight static implementation for Core Web Vitals.
- No official Cambridge logo or affiliation language outside required disclaimer.
- No reference to any parent company or non-public brand.

## 11. Facebook Ads Landing Page Recommendations

- Send cold traffic directly to `/igcse-diagnostic-test/`.
- Use the headline angle: "Find out exactly where your child is struggling before starting another tutoring class."
- Keep the offer low-friction: Diagnostic Test + Personal Study Roadmap.
- Avoid promising grade outcomes.
- Retarget visitors who view service pages but do not submit the form.
- Track form submissions, CTA clicks, scroll depth, and contact button clicks.
- Add real parent objections to the FAQ after the first few campaigns.
- Replace all placeholder contact links and the form endpoint before campaign launch.
