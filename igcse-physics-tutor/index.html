Physics gaps

Knowing formulas is not the same as solving Physics questions

IGCSE Physics often exposes gaps in method selection, unit conversion, graph interpretation, and written explanations. Diagnostic tutoring helps locate which part of the process is breaking down.

  • Formula selection and rearranging.
  • Units, standard form, and conversions.
  • Graph gradients, areas, and physical meaning.
  • Mechanics, energy, waves, electricity, and magnetism.
  • Command words for explanation and comparison questions.

FAQ

IGCSE Physics tutor questions

What Physics topics can be supported?

Support can cover mechanics, energy, waves, electricity, magnetism, thermal physics, radioactivity, space physics, units, formulas, and graphs.

Why do students struggle with IGCSE Physics past papers?

Many students can recall formulas but struggle to choose the right formula, convert units, interpret graphs, or explain reasoning in exam wording.

Can the diagnostic test check Physics only?

Yes. The diagnostic session can focus on Physics if that is the main concern.
