Chemistry gaps

IGCSE Chemistry requires both memory and reasoning

Students often memorise definitions but struggle when a question asks them to apply a concept, balance an equation, interpret an experiment, or explain a result using command-word logic.

  • Atomic structure, bonding, and periodic trends.
  • Chemical equations, moles, reacting masses, and formulas.
  • Acids, bases, salts, electrolysis, and rates of reaction.
  • Organic chemistry patterns and named reactions.
  • Practical skills, data interpretation, and explanation quality.

FAQ

IGCSE Chemistry tutor questions

What Chemistry areas are checked in the diagnostic test?

The diagnostic can check bonding, reactions, equations, moles, practical skills, organic chemistry, acids and bases, and command words.

Can IGCSE Lab help with moles and equations?

Yes. Many Chemistry students need structured rebuilding in equation balancing, reacting masses, molar relationships, and unit use.

Is Chemistry tutoring exam focused?

Tutoring connects concept rebuilding with exam-style questions, practical interpretation, and clear written explanations.
